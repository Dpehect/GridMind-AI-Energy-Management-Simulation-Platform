# GridMind 1.0 Release Candidate

GridMind is now a large-scale, local-first energy management and simulation platform.

Major capabilities:
- Energy operations dashboard
- Local intelligence and forecasting
- Scenario simulation
- 2D and 3D building visualization
- Tariff, cost and carbon intelligence
- Maintenance and device health
- Goals and recommendations
- Reporting Studio
- Enterprise roles and administration
- Analytics Suite
- Operations Suite
- PWA, offline mode, CI, Docker and portfolio showcase
