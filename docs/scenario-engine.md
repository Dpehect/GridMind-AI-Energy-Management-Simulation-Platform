# Scenario Simulation Engine

The Phase 10 simulation layer is deterministic and local-first. It calculates projected energy, cost and carbon outcomes from baseline metrics, implementation cost, expected reduction and confidence.

## Scoring

Scenario ranking combines effective reduction, confidence and payback performance. Results expose uncertainty through confidence and risk labels instead of presenting estimates as certainty.

## Supported interventions

- operating schedule optimization
- device upgrades
- tariff changes
- lighting retrofit
- night-load reduction
- peak shaving
- renewable generation
