# Marketing Experience

Phase 03 introduces a complete public product story with accessible motion and progressive enhancement.

## Motion ownership

- Framer Motion: component reveals and micro-interactions
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger: long-form editorial storytelling
- Lenis: optional smooth scrolling, disabled for reduced-motion users
- SplitType: scoped headline choreography
- Embla: accessible horizontal use-case browsing

Marketing motion must never block navigation, reading, or keyboard interaction.
